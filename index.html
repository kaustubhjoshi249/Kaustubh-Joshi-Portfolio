About Me

I am a Quality Engineer with over three years of experience, specializing in game testing and automation scripting for AAA titles. My expertise lies in developing robust test frameworks and enhancing test efficiency to minimize manual processes and ensure high-quality releases.

In my current role at Ubisoft, I conduct end-to-end testing for major HD games, where I've developed automation processes that reduced manual testing time by 40%. My work involves creating and optimizing test cases using frameworks like DTest and ensuring game stability by identifying and resolving critical issues.

My foundation in quality is built upon hands-on development experience as a Unity Game Developer. This background in creating game logic, multiplayer functionality, and AR/VR simulations gives me a deep understanding of the entire development lifecycle, allowing me to architect more effective and insightful testing solutions.
