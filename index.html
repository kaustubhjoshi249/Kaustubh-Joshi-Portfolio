Architect of Assured Realities

I architect reliable, human-centric digital experiences by blending rigorous quality assurance with innovative AR/VR and AI development.

With over four years of hands-on experience, I specialize in bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and user-centric design. My journey began with a formal education in Game Design, which I leveraged into a professional Unity AR/VR development role, building complex interactive simulations.

To create experiences that are not just functional but truly resonant, I studied Social Psychology. This unique lens allows me to approach development with a deep understanding of user perception and engagement.

This creative foundation is anchored by a robust background in both manual and automation QA. I ensure that every project—from GenAI tools to immersive games—is polished, performant, and reliable.
